# Hero Section Images

## Required Image

Place your hero section image here as:
- **hero.jpg** or **hero.jpeg** (1920x1080px recommended)

## Alternative Formats
You can also use:
- **hero.png** (if you need transparency)
- **hero.webp** (for better compression)

## Image Guidelines

### Recommended Specifications
- **Dimensions**: 1920x1080px (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPEG or WebP
- **File Size**: Under 500KB (optimized)
- **Subject**: Clean, modern home interior or professional cleaner in action

### Image Tips
1. **Composition**: Keep important elements in the center/left area (text appears on the left on desktop)
2. **Lighting**: Bright, well-lit images work best
3. **Colors**: Images with white, blue, or green tones match the brand colors
4. **Quality**: Use high-resolution images but compress them for web

### Where to Get Images
- **Unsplash**: https://unsplash.com/s/photos/cleaning-service
- **Pexels**: https://www.pexels.com/search/house-cleaning/
- **Custom Photography**: Professional photos of your team or work

## Current Status
The component will show a placeholder with emojis until you add hero.jpg to this folder.

## How to Add Your Image
1. Place your image in this folder: `/public/images/hero/`
2. Name it `hero.jpg` (or update the src in HeroSection.tsx)
3. Refresh your browser - the image will appear automatically!
