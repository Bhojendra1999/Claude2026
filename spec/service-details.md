## Section 8: FAQ

### Section Title
Frequently Asked Questions

### Questions (specific to each service)

**General Questions (all services):**

1. **Do I need to be home during the cleaning?**
   - No, many clients provide access and leave during service. We're fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind.

2. **What if I need to reschedule?**
   - We understand plans change! Just give us 24 hours notice to reschedule without any fees.

3. **Do you bring your own supplies?**
   - Yes, we provide all cleaning supplies and equipment. If you prefer specific products, we're happy to use them.

**Service-Specific Questions:**

*Regular Cleaning:*
- **How often should I schedule regular cleaning?**
  - Most clients prefer weekly or bi-weekly. We can customize frequency to your needs.

*Deep Cleaning:*
- **How is deep cleaning different from regular cleaning?**
  - Deep cleaning is more intensive and includes areas not covered in regular cleaning, like inside appliances, baseboards, and detailed work.

*Move In/Out:*
- **When should I schedule move-in/out cleaning?**
  - Ideally after all furniture is removed but before final walkthrough.
