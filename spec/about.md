## Section 2: Our Story

### Section Title
Our Story

### Content (3-4 paragraphs)

> **How It Started**
> Founded in 2015, our cleaning service began with a simple mission: to provide professional, reliable cleaning services that busy families and professionals can trust. What started as a small team of dedicated cleaners has grown into a full-service cleaning company serving hundreds of satisfied clients across the region.

> **What Drives Us**
> We believe everyone deserves to come home to a clean, comfortable space without the stress of cleaning themselves. Our passion for excellence and attention to detail sets us apart. Every surface we clean, every room we transform, is a reflection of our commitment to quality.

> **Where We Are Today**
> Today, we're proud to be one of the most trusted cleaning services in the area. Our team has grown, our services have expanded, but our core values remain the same: reliability, quality, and customer satisfaction. We've cleaned over 10,000 homes and counting!

> **Looking Ahead**
> As we continue to grow, we remain committed to providing the same personalized service that our early customers experienced. We're constantly improving our methods, using the latest eco-friendly products, and training our team to deliver exceptional results.

- Include real photos when possible (team, office, cleaning in action)
- Stats should be accurate and verifiable
- Certifications should be real and current
- Testimonials should feel authentic (include full names, locations)
- Keep tone professional but warm and personal
- Mobile-responsive layout with proper image optimization
- Consider adding a video introduction (future enhancement)
- Link to relevant service pages where appropriate
- Include schema markup for LocalBusiness and Organization
- Add FAQ section if space permits
